Integrating All Financial Assets

January 2023

Many people dream of achieving financial freedom, where they no longer have to worry about their financial condition. ü•∞

The goal of this project is to design a user experience that makes it easy for users to manage all of their financial assets in one place and make informed investment decisions.

To understand the needs of potential users, user research was conducted through surveys, interviews, and focus groups. The research revealed that users struggle with keeping track of their various financial assets and understanding the performance of their investments. They also express a desire for a single platform that can provide a holistic view of their financial situation and suggest investment opportunities.

Context
üìù Overview

This case study is about the development of a financial management platform that aims to help users integrate all of their financial assets in one place and make it easier for them to manage and track their investments.

üòé My Role

As the designer, I was responsible for designing the UI, UX, and rendering layouts. I created frameworks and prototypes to share the vision, design principles, and section strategy. This helped to conceptualize ideas, gain alignment, and drive decision making.

‚è≥ Timeline

Over the 1 months timeline, so I decided to use a Design Thinking process that can solve the problem in 1 months, as I had 2 week for the research and then 2 week design development.

Problem
‚ö°Ô∏è The Challenge:

How to help users integrating all financial assets to achieve financial freedom

    Based on the research, the main problems that users face are:
  • Difficulty keeping track of multiple financial assets
  • Difficulty understanding the performance of their investments
  • Lack of a holistic view of their financial situation
  • Difficulty identifying investment opportunities
Design Process
üìö Research

My first step was to understand the constraints of Finaz users, The results of user research found that 5 main problems

  • Many users find it difficult to keep track of multiple financial assets and have trouble understanding the performance of their investments.
  • Many users want a single platform that can provide a holistic view of their financial situation and suggest investment opportunities.
  • Many users struggle with budgeting and cash flow management, and find it hard to understand where their money is going.
  • Many users find current financial management tools confusing and difficult to use.
  • Many users express a desire for more personalized investment recommendations.

These findings would inform the design of the financial management platform by highlighting the key pain points and needs of the users, such as the need for a single platform that provides a holistic view of their financial situation, the need for investment recommendations that are personalized and easy to understand, and the need for an easy-to-use website solution.

Design

A holistic view

The platform would allow users to connect all of their financial accounts, such as bank accounts, investment accounts, and retirement accounts, and provide a clear, easy-to-understand view of the performance of the user's investments. This feature would help users better understand their overall financial situation, and allow them to make more informed investment decisions.

The header is inspired by the chart as a representative of growing assets

Design

AI Personalized Investments

The platform would use the user's financial situation and risk tolerance to suggest investment opportunities that are tailored to their specific needs. This feature would help users identify potential investment opportunities that align with their goals and risk tolerance, and make it easier for them to grow their wealth.

Results

After much deliberation, I came up with a page designed with a sense of clarity and purpose of navigation that can be accessed efficiently, So I decided to create a financial management platform that allows users to connect all of their financial accounts, such as bank accounts, investment accounts, and retirement accounts. The platform would provide a clear, easy-to-understand view of the performance of the user's investments and suggest investment opportunities based on their financial situation and risk tolerance. Users would also be able to set financial goals and track their progress towards achieving them.

Takeaways
‚ú® Develop Design Craft
    I have learned several things about developing a financial management platform.
  1. A user-centered approach is essential for creating a platform that effectively meets the needs of the users. By conducting user research and gathering feedback, we can ensure that the final solution addresses the users' pain points and helps them achieve their goals.
  2. A holistic view of financial assets and personalized investment recommendations are important features that can help users make more informed investment decisions and manage their finances more effectively.
  3. Design Thinking process is a valuable approach for solving complex problem, like this one, as it allows for flexibility, iteration and user-centered approach.
üåü Practice Makes Perfect!

I learned a lot while designing this landing page, not only developing my problem solving skills and user interface convenience, but I also increasingly understood that by using the financial management platform regularly, users will become more proficient and confident in managing their finances. The more they use the platform, the more they will understand its features and capabilities, and the more effectively they will be able to manage their financial assets. Additionally, as the platform is refined and improved over time based on user feedback, users will have access to an increasingly user-friendly and effective tool for managing their finances.

It's also important to note that, by integrating with other financial apps and services, the platform will provide a more complete picture of the user's financial situation, which can help users better understand their financial situation and make more informed decisions.
